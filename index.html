our process

Creative Process

As a team of people obsessed with quality, having a well defined end to end process for taking on any problem is of utmost importance to us! And it also helps us establish a deeper connection with our partners and clients.

Project Definition

The very first and most quintessential step of any successful product/project is defining and documenting all the aspects of its development.

Product and UX Research

Once the scope of the project is defined, the next important step is to create the best possible user experience for the product.

Product Development

Once all the design is done, and the product looks as good as it can and has the best user experience defined for it, the process of developing and coding finally starts!

Wireframes and Design

This step involves translating all the research and insights collected in the previous step, into unique and eye-catching designs/wire frames.

Product Testing

Developing the product by itself is not enough. Continuous testing and validation is required at each and every step to esure, that we are creating a robust and well rounded application/product.

Deployment Solutions

This is the step where the product finally goes live . Deploying the product to the internet/app store/play store for everyone to use!

Product Maintenance

This is the step which binds everything together. Once the product goes live, there are several feedbacks and issues that might come, and all of that is encompassed in this final step.
